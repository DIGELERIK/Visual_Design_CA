Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I book a tutoring session?

A session can be booked through our website, you select your chosen topic, tutor and your preferred time slot. Payment details are handled securely during the booking process.

Do you offer in-person tutoring or only online sessions?

Currently, we only offer online tutoring by video call. This is the most convenient way for us to provide seamless and efficient tutoring sessions.

Do I need any special software or equipment for online sessions?

For our tutoring sessions, you will need a device with internet access (e.g. laptop, PC), a working webcam and microphone will also be needed. We use these for our video conferences. We will provide guidance on what specific software’s will be required for each lesson!

How can I prepare for my first session?

To make the most of your first session, it's helpful to:

- Identify the topics you want to cover or specific questions you have.

- Have access to any necessary software or coding environments for hands-on practice.

- Be prepared to share your goals, challenges, and any previous knowledge on the subject. Our tutors will guide you through the rest!

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session?

Our lessons can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance! If cancelled in less than 24 hours before a session, a small fee may apply. We understand that sometimes life can get in the way!

Can I receive a certification for completing a course?

Yes, three out of six of our courses will provide you with a certification. For more information about our courses check out our courses offered page! Courses Offered page.

Are the tutoring sessions personalized?

Every one of our tutoring sessions are designed and customised to help you reach and meet your specific goals! We take into account that everyone has different learning paces. Whether you’re a beginner or someone looking for a way to advance your skills that already have a strong foundation, Tech tutors is the way to go!

What is your refund policy?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee, if after your first session you find you’re not happy, we will first offer to schedule you with one of our other tutors or refund you for all remaining sessions!
